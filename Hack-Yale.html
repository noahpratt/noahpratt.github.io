Y-Hack, Bias

At Y-Hack, my team and I made Bias, a mobile platform which scans a users' picture of a logo, searches online, and returns positive and negative news surrounding the company, revealing the true nature of their bias. This project was created over an intense 36-hour hackathon at Yale University.
