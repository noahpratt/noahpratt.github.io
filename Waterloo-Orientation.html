UWaterloo Orientation 2019

The University of Waterloo Fall Orientation is the annual welcome week for around 2000 incoming first year students. As lead videographer, I supported the media team from the Student Success Office, responsible for capturing the events for all faculties.

The details

  • Goal: Lead videography team, facilitate event shoots, coordinate footage for editing, showing off all that orientation has to offer.
  • Team: Celia (Media Team Coordinator?), Jen (Student Success Supervisor?), Saphra (Student Success assistant?)
  • Audience: Prospective and current university students
  • Duration: May - September 2019
  • Skills: Videography, Team Leadership, Adobe Premiere Pro, Managing tight deadlines.
