The World Leadership Congress (WLC) hosts around 400 students annually in Chicago, IL, to celebrate and empower the leaders of tomorrow. As design manager, I created the branding concepts, as well as many other deliverables for the event. Pictured to the left, is the event logo.

Blockbreaker Game

This is a blockbreaker type game that I coded using Processing. The user controlls the paddle with the keyboard, and tries to hit all of the blocks. The colours are a nod to the classic, retro gaming aesthetic.
