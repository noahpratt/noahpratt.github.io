Trualta, Junior Designer

Trualta is an education healthcare startup which helps family caregivers provide care to their aging loved ones. As the Junior Designer for Trualta, I've collaborated with all of the staff to keep branding on track, test and develop product opportunities, and organize content to be shared to client portals. One of my key projects has been creating customizable marketing kits for partner agencies to effectively communicate the benefits Trualta can make in their lives.
