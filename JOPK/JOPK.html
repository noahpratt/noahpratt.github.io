Welcome to Journey of the Pairie King!

This is a game I coded a couple years back for class to practice game design and challenge my coding abilities.

Journey of the Prairie King is a mini-game from the 2016 game, Stardew Valley.

In this game, you play as the hero on a valiant quest to save their village from attacking enemies!

Modeled after twin-stick arcade machines, Journey of the Prairie King is a top-down shooter, and a throwback to the classics of arcades.


Back Home

Here are some instructions on how to play the game:

  • The WASD keys move your player around the screen
  • The arrow keys shoot bullets
  • Enemies will come towards you from all sides of the field

If you can survive long enough, you'll win!
